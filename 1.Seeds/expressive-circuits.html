Emotionally Expressive Circuits

What it is

A fusion of sensor input and actuator response to express internal states or 'feelings' through movement or light.

Basic principle

Takes sensor input (light, proximity, touch) and maps it to actuator behavior (servo position, LED pattern) that reflects emotional states.
